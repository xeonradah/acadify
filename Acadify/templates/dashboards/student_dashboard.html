Dean's List Information

The Dean's List is a recognition of academic excellence awarded to students who meet the following criteria:

Maintain a GWA of 1.75 or better (Academic subjects only)

No failing grades (5.00) in Academic subjects

No grade below 2.00 in Academic subjects

18+ total units enrolled

Non-Academic subjects (PE, NSTP) are still required but don't affect GWA computation

Regular student with Block Section status

No INC/AW/UW marks

Only Academic subjects count towards Dean's List
